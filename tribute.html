Tom Waits

"They say I have no hits and that I'm difficult to work with... like it's a bad thing."

Notable Events

  • Tom was born in Pomona, California on December 7, 1949.
  • He released his first album, Closing Time, in 1973.
  • Tom made his first film apperance in Paradise Alley, starring Sylvester Stallone, in 1978.
  • In 1980, Tom married Kathleen Brennan, with whom he would colaborate.
  • With theater director Robert Wilson and beat poet William S. Burroughs, he produced the musical play The Black Rider, in 1989.
  • Tom Waits was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2011.
  • In 2015, Rolling Stone magazine placed him at #55 on their list of "100 Greatest Songwriters of All Time".
  • The last studio album released by Mr. Waits was 2011's Bad As Me
  • Tom used to come into the Barnes and Noble bookstore I worked at in Santa Rosa, California. He once asked me to throw away his Starbucks cup. He was lovely and charming, leaning across the counter, asking if I had anywhere I could "throw away an old coffee cup". He was absolutely, perfectly, Tom.
